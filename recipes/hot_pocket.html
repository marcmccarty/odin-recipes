Hot Pocket

Description

Hot Pockets are tasty and easy to prepare!

This simple recipe, if you can call it that, will involve a small amount of origami, if you can call it that.

Ingredients

  • Hot Pocket

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F
  2. When the oven has pre-heated, take your Hot Pocket and its cardboard sleeve out of the plastic packaging
  3. Get out a baking sheet, place aluminum foil over its surface, and then place the Hot Pocket on top of the foil
  4. Place the baking sheet in the oven and set a timer for 30 minutes
  5. Fold the cardboard sleeve with the printed instructions
  6. When the timer's up, let the Hot Pocket sit for a few minutes and then place it into the cardboard sleeve
  7. Enjoy your Hot Pocket!
