Peanut Butter and Jam (PB&J) Sandwich

Description

The PB&J sandwich is a classic staple for people of all walks of life in the USA.

This simple recipe will get you started on your journey to cook all sorts of delicious dishes!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jam

Steps

  1. Place two slices of bread on a plate
  2. Spread peanut butter onto one slice of bread with a butter knife
  3. Spread jam onto the other slice of bread with a clean butter knife
  4. Flip the peanut butter slice onto the jam slice so that the peanut butter and jam sides touch
  5. Enjoy your PB&J!
