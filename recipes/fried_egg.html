Fried Egg

Description

Fried eggs are tasty and easy to make. Let's get started!

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Butter (or an alternative non-stick solution)

Steps

  1. Put a small pat of butter on a frying pan
  2. Heat the pan to medium or medium-low heat
  3. When the butter has melted, crack an egg onto the frying pan
  4. Heat until the egg has cooked enough to no longer be liquid
  5. Flip the egg and wait 30 seconds to 1 minute, depending on preference
  6. Enjoy your fried egg!
